Privacy Policy

Irradiated Solution ("we", "us", or "our") operates the CarLoanMateAU mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our App.

CarLoanMateAU does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information to external servers. All financial calculations are performed entirely on your device.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, address)
  • Financial account details or banking information
  • Location data
  • Device contacts or photos
  • Health or biometric data

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The following information is stored locally on your device using Apple's standard UserDefaults and never transmitted to us:

  • Saved calculation scenarios (car price, loan details, salary inputs)
  • App preferences and settings
  • Premium purchase status

Third-Party Services

Google AdMob (Free Version Only)

The free version of CarLoanMateAU displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve relevant ads. This data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. Premium users who have purchased the unlock are not shown advertisements.

For more information: Google Privacy Policy

Apple StoreKit (In-App Purchases)

In-app purchases are processed entirely by Apple. We do not receive or store your payment information. Purchase transactions are subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

App Tracking Transparency

CarLoanMateAU may request permission to track your activity for the purpose of displaying relevant advertisements (free version only). You may decline this request and the app will continue to function normally with non-personalised ads.

Children's Privacy

CarLoanMateAU is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
